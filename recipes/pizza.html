Pizza

Description

It's pizza. Stuff on bread.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Stuff you want on your pizza

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, water, yeast and salt in the correct proportion.
  2. Wait until it becomes dough.
  3. Stretch it into a pizza base.
  4. Put your toppings on and bake.
